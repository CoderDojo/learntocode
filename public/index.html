Help

DIV

Divs help you style things and put them where you want on your webpage.

I have put 'div#styling' in my code but you don't have to put the word 'styling' in you can put whatever you want to call that div.

	
  <div id="boxArea">
  </div>

div#styling {
	color: pink;
}

div#styling {
	position: absolute;
	color: red;
	top: 100px;
	left: 105px;
}

'Position: absolute;' allows you to put something in a position. so remember to put it in.

Thanks to Niamh

A key ethos of CoderDojo is to pass on and share the programming knowledge you gain. Niamh has really taken to this aim in CoderDojo and is a key mentor at CoderDojo Girls. Not content with only mentoring, for Coolest Projects 2013 Niamh created a website to teach others how to program. Inspiring vision and generosity from someone of her age.

Niamh's coolest project is the foundation for this website and at CoderDojo DCU we have worked with Niamh help to take her project to the next level. Thanks Niamh for your insight and work to make this website possible along with sharing your passion for coding with anyone looking to learn.
